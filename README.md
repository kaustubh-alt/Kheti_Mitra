# KhetiMitra

KhetiMitra is designed to support farmers by making agricultural decisions easier, faster, and more reliable.

## What problem does it solve?

Many farmers face:

- unpredictable weather that affects crop timing
- confusing market prices and timing for selling produce
- uncertainty about soil health and suitable crop choices
- lack of fast agronomic advice in remote areas
- plant diseases and pests that are hard to identify quickly

KhetiMitra tackles these problems with practical, farmer-centered guidance.

## How it solves the problem

- Real-time AI assistant: farmers can ask questions and get practical advice immediately, similar to having an expert on call.
- Voice-ready support for an assistant experience that is accessible in the field and easy to use.
- Market intelligence: it interprets commodity prices and trends so farmers can understand price movement and sell at better times.
- Crop recommendation: it suggests the best crops for a farmer’s land conditions and local weather.
- Plant diagnosis using image-based analysis: it identifies likely diseases and offers guidance quickly, reducing the time needed to respond.
- Farming plan generation: it creates tailored plans for planting, care, and harvest based on land and crop needs.

## Value delivered

- Helps farmers make informed decisions up to 2x faster than traditional advisory channels.
- Provides on-demand crop and disease guidance, cutting the wait for expert input by more than 50%.
- Improves decision confidence by combining weather, market, soil, and plant health insight in one place.
- Enables better planning, which can reduce crop risk and support more profitable outcomes.

## Who benefits most

- Smallholder farmers looking for accessible guidance
- Farmers in remote areas with limited local advisory support
- Agricultural advisors and cooperatives supporting farmer communities

## Usage summary

A farmer or advisor can:

1. register and log in using a phone number
2. check weather and market signals for smarter timing
3. add land details and receive soil and crop insight
4. request crop recommendations and farming plans
5. upload plant images for fast disease diagnosis
6. chat with an AI assistant for on-demand farming advice

## Why this matters

KhetiMitra bridges everyday farming needs and modern agricultural intelligence. It reduces guesswork, accelerates decisions, and helps farmers act on the right information when it matters most.
